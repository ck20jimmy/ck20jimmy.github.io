Chih-Chun (Jimmy) Yang

I am a master student majoring in computer vision from the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Fernando De la Torre in the Human Sensing Lab.

Prior to enrolling at CMU, I obtained an M.S. in computer science from National Taiwan University (國立台灣大學), where I was advised by Prof. Shou-De Lin in the Machine Discovery and Social Network Mining Lab (MSLab) and Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang in the Vision and Learning Lab (VLLab).

I'm passionate and experienced in generative-AI. My research interests focus on cross-modal learning and 3d vision, particularly vision-audio, vision-language, and 3d human motion topics.
